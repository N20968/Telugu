Our Story

The Taste of
Telugu Home

Welcome to Teluginti Ruchulu — where every plate tells the story of Andhra Pradesh's rich culinary heritage. Located in the heart of KPHB Colony, Kukatpally, we've been serving authentic Telugu meals that remind you of home.

From our spice-laden biryanis to our comforting full-meal thalis, every dish is prepared fresh daily using traditional recipes passed down through generations.
